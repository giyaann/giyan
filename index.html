Areas of Opportunity

Your feedback helps us grow! Here's what we're working on:

Advertisements/Promotions and Live Stream Design

+

"Most viewers said the changes were better, but some were conflicted."

  • Improved advertisements and stream design compared to the first stream
  • Majority preferred the new style, but some viewers were unsure
Action Plan: Continue refining promotional materials and gather more targeted feedback for future improvements.

Chosen Games

+

"Some parts were good, some were boring. Wish we could join the games."

  • Changed games from the first stream
  • Some viewers enjoyed the new games, others found parts boring
  • Requests for more interactive games with audience participation
Action Plan: Include more interactive games and allow viewers to join in future streams.

Pacing and Time Management

+

"The stream was too long and less engaging near the end."

  • Stream duration exceeded 3 hours and 30 minutes
  • Viewers suggested a shorter stream for better engagement
  • Fewer viewers remained towards the end
Action Plan: Plan for a more concise stream and monitor engagement to optimize timing.

Technology

+

"Would love to see your reactions on face cam for more connection."

  • Requests for face cam to see streamers' reactions
  • Not all members could use cams due to internet issues
  • Lag occurred when cams were enabled
Action Plan: Improve internet setup and test face cams before the stream to ensure smooth experience.
